Terms and Conditions of Entry

By entering the Rajiv Gandhi Zoological Park, you agree to abide by the following rules and regulations. Failure to comply may result in a fine, removal from the premises, or other penalties as per the Indian Wildlife (Protection) Act 1972.

It is important that every visitor takes care to not disturb or harm the animals in any way. Violations of these rules are considered a serious offense.

Prohibited Activities

  • Feeding, injuring, or disturbing any animal.
  • Littering inside the zoo premises.
  • Carrying narcotics, liquor, tobacco, or any smoking substances.
  • Bringing explosives, inflammatory items, firearms, or any kind of weapon.
  • Playing loud music or any musical instruments.
  • Cooking food or lighting fires for any purpose.
  • Bringing air-filled balloons, balls, or other sports equipment.
  • Entry for pet animals is strictly prohibited.
  • Damaging any zoo property.

Payment Modes Available

We accept the following modes of payment for online ticket booking and at our counters:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, Rupay)
  • Net Banking
  • UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm)
  • Cash (at counter only)

Ticket Cancellation and Refund Policy

Tickets once booked are **non-refundable** and **non-transferable**. Please review your booking details carefully before making the payment. We do not offer refunds or exchanges for any reason, including but not limited to weather conditions, change of plans, or any other unforeseen circumstances.

However, if the park authorities cancel or close the park on your booked date due to an unavoidable event, a full refund will be processed to the original mode of payment within 7-10 business days. We will notify you via the email address provided during booking.

Important Information

  • Entry tickets are valid for a single entry and expire upon exiting the zoo.
  • Free entry is provided for disabled persons upon showing valid ID.
  • Battery-operated car services are subject to availability.
  • Wheelchairs are available with a refundable deposit of ₹100.
  • Please cooperate with zoo staff at all times.
